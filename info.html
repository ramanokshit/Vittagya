About Our App

Mission and Vision

Welcome to Vittagya: The Financial Advisor Platform, an initiative by Team SAVN to empower rural women with personalized financial tools, education, and resources. Our platform is a beacon of financial independence, combining cutting-edge technology with an intuitive design to simplify money management and promote entrepreneurial growth. Our mission is to bridge the gap in financial literacy for women in underserved communities by providing an accessible, multilingual platform. We aim to equip users with the knowledge and tools necessary to make informed financial decisions, improve their quality of life, and inspire a culture of confidence, collaboration, and entrepreneurship. Together, Team SAVN from Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, is dedicated to creating a platform that transforms financial challenges into opportunities, paving the way for a brighter and more secure future for rural women.
